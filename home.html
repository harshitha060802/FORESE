About Us


FORESE (FORum for Economic Studies by Engineers) is a student-run forum that aims at assisting engineers in seizing placement opportunities through a wide array of preparatory events. FORESE equips engineering students with all the skills they need to excel in the workplace and trace the path to an ideal career.

Coders' Forum & The Wealth Society are the two wings of FORESE. Coders' Forum believes in the motto of students helping students. It aspires to break the barriers faced by students when learning to code. The Wealth Society promotes financial knowledge among students and offers help to achieve financial literacy and manage the financial challenges that life throws at them.
