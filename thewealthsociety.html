About The Wealth Society


The Wealth Society is a wing of FORESE that promotes financial knowledge among students and offers help to achieve financial literacy and manage the financial challenges that life throws at them. We hope to provide an opportunity for students to improve their understanding of the intricacies of the world of finance and foster an environment where they can learn, share, participate, organize and network with the industry.
